Indiscriminate Exposure

Passive international indexing forces investors into indiscriminate exposure across companies and geographies, including businesses that no disciplined value investor would own individually.

State-Owned Enterprises

Government-controlled entities with misaligned incentives and opaque governance structures.

Low-Margin Conglomerates

Sprawling businesses with poor capital allocation and limited competitive advantages.

Poor Governance

Companies with weak shareholder protections and management accountability issues.

The Solution: Disciplined Concentration

We own 18-20 exceptional companies selected through rigorous fundamental analysis, avoiding the pitfalls of passive indexing.

  • Knowability: We invest only in businesses we can understand deeply, with transparent operations and predictable cash flows.
  • Active Share: Our concentrated portfolio looks nothing like the index, providing true diversification and alpha generation potential.
